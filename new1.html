Who Is Major Sreekumar?

The story of Major Sreekumar, played by Jayaram, is a compelling narrative that intertwines domesticity with the complexities of a military past. Sreekumar, portrayed as a loving husband and skilled domestic worker, is thrust into the spotlight when Colonel Tomy Eapen Devassy, played by Suresh Gopi, enters his life. This unexpected arrival disrupts the tranquility of Sreekumar's domestic life, revealing his past as a highly skilled military officer. The film delves into the hidden truths of both Sreekumar and Suja, Suja's real identity as Leena Jacob, and the challenges they face as they confront their pasts. The climax sees Sreekumar embracing his old self and taking down the threats, while Suja reveals her true self, showcasing their acceptance of each other's past and their journey to finding true love.
